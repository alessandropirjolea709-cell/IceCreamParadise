Health and safety

At our ice cream business, the health and safety of our customers is our highest priority. We are committed to providing a safe, clean, and hygienic environment where everyone can enjoy our delicious ice cream with confidence. All of our products are made using fresh, high-quality ingredients, carefully sourced and inspected to ensure they meet strict safety standards. Every step of our ice cream preparation—from mixing and freezing to serving—is carried out under rigorous hygiene practices. Our staff is fully trained in food safety procedures, including proper handwashing, sanitization of equipment and surfaces, safe handling of ingredients, and correct storage temperatures for all products.

We take special care to prevent cross-contamination, particularly for customers with food allergies or dietary restrictions, including gluten-free, nut-free, and dairy-free options. Our equipment and serving tools are regularly cleaned and maintained, and we follow strict protocols for labeling and storage to ensure freshness and safety. Additionally, our store environment is kept clean and welcoming at all times, with attention to both customer areas and back-of-house operations. By prioritizing health and safety alongside quality and flavor, we aim to create not only a delicious ice cream experience but also peace of mind for our customers. Every scoop we serve is a combination of taste, care, and commitment to your well-being.
